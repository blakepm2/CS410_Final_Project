According to the BLS, the highest-paying computer science jobs belong to computer and information research scientists and information systems managers, who as of 2021 make median annual wages of $131,490 and $159,010, respectively. What fun jobs can I get with a computer science degree? While fun is subjective, computer science degrees can lead to many exciting careers.
